Assembling three albums into a journey through life, death, and the afterlife, the Flying Lotus Box Set uses digital collage and typographic texture to explore the sounds and influences of Steven Ellison's music.

In an attempt to visually mimic the audial and emotional textures of Ellison's work, the catalog utilizes vivid collages of imagery and type to walk the viewer through the evolving emotions of Los Angeles, Cosmogramma, and You're Dead.

The physical form of the catalog itself references the layering of influences and sounds through the use of different page sizes that obscure and highlight text and image at curated points.
