The Land Rover Burghley Horse Trials are an annual three-day equestrian eventing competition held on the grounds of Burghley House in England.

It exists as one of the UK's most attended sporting occasions, with guests ranging from international equestrian enthusiasts to members of the British Royal Family. It needed a website that capitalized on the luxury of its setting, providing an experience that persuaded and informed visitors year round.

The redesign seeks to use thoughtful photography paired with refined typography to express the high-end nature of the event. It provides a simplified and reorganized information architecture that allows visitors to easily plan their experience.
