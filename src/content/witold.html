The Witold Gombrowicz Collection assembles five of the famed Polish author's most celebrated works. It is currently a work in progress.

The book covers rely exclusively on expressive typographic form—inspired by Polish poster design—in order to express the abstract and absurd nature of Gombrowicz's writing.

The interiors of the book convey my typesetting abilities through careful attention to detail, while still referencing the concept of juvenility as expressed on the covers.
