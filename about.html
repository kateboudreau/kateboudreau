Hello! I'm Kate Boudreau, a graphic designer anticipating graduation from Massachusetts College of Art and Design in May 2017 with a BFA in Graphic Design.

I began my education at Connecticut College in New London, Connecticut, where I pursued an interest in the intersection between computer science and fine arts, as well as developed my skills as a analytical thinker and writer. Eventually, I moved on to RISD's SIGDS, and finally, to MassArt, allowing me to build a strong foundation in graphic design, with a focus on how it can be used to build innovative web experiences.

Most recently, I worked at Upstatement as a Design Apprentice. This opportunity not only allowed me to gain experience collaborating with a multi-disciplinary team, but taught me an enormous amount about front-end development and designing for digital platforms.

I'm currently exploring what comes next in my graphic design career. Have a look at my résumé, and let's chat!
